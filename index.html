TWITCH REWIND

For the past few years, the Twitch platform has seen over hundreds of games that became hot and trending. While some games have died out as a fad and some continue to dominate the charts on Twitch, we show the larger picture of what types of genres the users will continue to watch. The heatmap below shows the genres of the top 200 games every month from 2016 to 2021.

Annotations

  1. Tactical/Shooter - April 7th 2020: Valorant open Beta starts. Afterwards, the Tactical and Shooter genres are slightly darker after this date due to the influx of new Valorant streamers.

  2. Indie - August/September of 2020: The indie game Among Us reaches peak levels of hype. Afterwards, Indie viewership stays slightly elevated from previous levels.

  3. Puzzle - September 18th 2020: Investigative horror game Phasmophobia is released. Afterwards, Puzzle viewership stays slightly elevated from previous levels.

  4. Platformer - May 16, 2020: Terraria Journey's End update. This was a huge final update to the popular platformer Terraria. Prior to this date, the last major update to Terraria was in 2016. There is a brief spike for just this month of May.
